Tank Healer archetype — a skill-tree build that stacks the healer's armor (and maybe a self-ward passive) so it becomes the party's meat shield. This needs almost no new combat code: PartyAI.kiteGoal already ranks shields by toughness = armor*ARMOR_WEIGHT + hp and tucks ranged units behind the toughest, with the healer as the existing fallback. Enough armor on the healer makes it win that ranking, so the squishy backline (archers) automatically forms up behind the medic — emergent comedy, formation inverts with zero special-casing. Twist on the theme: you can't order the party to protect you, but you can become the thing worth protecting behind. Belongs as a branch under skilltree (skilltree-t3i2). Knobs: armor-raising nodes, self-ward passive (wards already absorb before HP in applyDamage), maybe a heal-on-being-hit node.
