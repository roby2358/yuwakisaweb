    TIGER! TIGER!
    
    
    You are Gully Foyle — mechanic's mate 3rd class, education none, skills none,
    merits none. For 170 days you rotted in the airless wreck of the Nomad.
    Then the liner Vorga saw your flares... and passed you by.
    
    
    Now you are back on the scarred earth, and five of Vorga's crew are hiding on this map.
    Find them. Make them talk. Learn who gave the order.
    
    
      
      
    • Walk (arrows / WASD / click adjacent) — every tile you walk is memorized.

    • Jaunte (click any memorized tile) — instant teleport, but the shockwave alerts every hunter within 7 tiles.
      • 
      
    • Rage — wounds and confessions stoke it. At full rage the tiger stigmata blazes: 3 actions, jaunte to anything you can see... and every hunter knows exactly where you are.

    • Radiation ☢ burns you but hunters won't follow. The wreck ⌂ heals you. Informants ? sell locations. PyrE ✶ is the fire that ends worlds — it answers only to a burning mind.
