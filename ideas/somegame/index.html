EMBERGRADE

A wildfire is spreading across the island. Command three firefighters and save every village for 15 days.

Click a firefighter to select.
Move: click a yellow hex.
Dig (2 MP): turn the firefighter's hex into a firebreak — fire cannot cross it.
Douse (3 MP): extinguish flames on the firefighter's hex and all six neighbors.
End Day to let the fire spread.

Lose if any village burns. Win by surviving 15 days.
