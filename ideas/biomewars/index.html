Biome Wars

This planet is at war with itself. Living biomes grind against each other hex by hex — some lush, some inhospitable, some outright hostile to you — and the creatures that wander them never cross a front line.

You are one small hero. Gather essence (hostile land pays best), learn talents, feed the settlements that shelter you, and cleanse the blights that drive the enemy tide. The war never ends. Thrive anyway.
