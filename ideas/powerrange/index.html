You command a city-state (gold). Cannon platforms rule the open field; the only counter is shields — and the cheap, fast Shield Knight that walks through the fire to disable and capture the enemy's war machines.

It's an economy. Hold gold hexes for income, spend it on units, ammo, and upkeep. Capture the enemy Foundry or break their treasury to win.

Keep a unit beside a platform to escort it — an escorted machine can't be sieged. Knights crack only undefended ones.
