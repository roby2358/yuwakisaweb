Solar Corsair

You are a swashbuckling pirate captain adrift in a contested star sector. Loot 3 derelict hulks ($), then escape through the wormhole (◉) on the far side of the sector.

  • Imperial Freighters (F) drift the lanes. Board one for 1 hull (86% success): 3-7 doubloons on a clean take.
  • Dreadnoughts (D) hunt you across the sector. Boarding one costs 1 hull and is a coin flip — but pays 10 doubloons and removes the threat.
  • Nebulae conceal your ship from the Dreadnought — it loses your trail and drifts.
  • Asteroid fields and nebulae are slow but the Dreadnought cannot pursue through stars or void rifts.
  • Trade Stations (T) repair 1 hull for 2 doubloons when you stop on them.

Right-click drag to pan. Click your ship to plot a course.
