The Night of the Simoor

The desert wind blows and the Sapphire Court throws its masked revel. The hall is a sea of identical veiled blanks — impostors, revelers, and the hidden Veiled Sovereign, all alike. Nobody is readable until you claim a Favor (❀): that opens the court, and the impostors and the Sovereign all show as ?.

And the insufferable Vicomte de Vavoom wants the Sovereign too — and he starts at your elbow. Claim what you can before he does.
