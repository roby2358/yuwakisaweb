AUGUR

Your visions are always true and never complete. Something red-toothed is coming — but where? The mill is doomed — but when? You are the village augur, and the future arrives in facets: what, where, when, who, how hard.

Divine to know more, and carry the weight of knowing. Raise wards where you dare to guess. Warn the village and spend its patience. Fame will find you — and fame draws grander dooms. There is no ending and no defeat: dooms land, stones are raised, the vale endures, and so do you.
