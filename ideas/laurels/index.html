The Ladder

LAURELS

Rest on them and be forgotten.

You are a hero whose legend must be fed. Every dawn the bards' interest wanes — renown melts by a tenth — and rank, title, and privilege go with it. The only fuel is deeds.

Acquire: harvest forage, ore, and relics (the dread pays best), hunt beasts, and carry it all home — nothing counts until it's banked. Rise: bank hauls, raise monuments, climb from Unsung toward Mythic. Destroy: topple named dooms for windfalls of glory — or sack a holding, if you can bear the name. The world keeps score, and it answers.

You cannot lose. You can only be forgotten.
