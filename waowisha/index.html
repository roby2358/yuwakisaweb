Turn 1

Waowisha

The land hums with resources nobody fully understands. Things stir at the edges. Your job is to keep The Loom alive.

Your Goal

Fulfill The Mandate — a series of manufacturing objectives. Build production chains, gather resources, and produce what's asked before the Drift overwhelms you.

Getting Started
  • Click your Warden (W) to select, then click a yellow hex to move
  • Click your unit again after selecting to see Build and Recruit options
  • Move to Scarp (brown) terrain and stay there — your Warden gathers 1 resource per turn
  • You start with enough to recruit a Gatherer — click your Warden on The Loom
  • Spacebar or Enter ends your turn
The Basics
  • Gather raw resources from colored terrain (Vein, Grove, Mire, Scarp)
  • Build Refineries, Foundries, and Ateliers to process resources into higher-tier products
  • Defend with Sentinels (mobile) and towers (Spikes, Watch Posts, etc.)
  • The Drift spawns enemies from map edges — they grow fast if ignored
  • Right-click drag to pan the map
Every game generates new names for resources, products, and enemies. Check the HUD to learn what things are called this time.
