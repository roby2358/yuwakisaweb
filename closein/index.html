Welcome to Close In!

The point of this game is to be the player who can get closest to a number without going over.

Be the first to earn 6 round points to win the game!

Each turn you can Roll or Stop.

  • If you go over, you lose ONE POINT!
  • If you win, you get ONE POINT!
  • If you get the number exactly, you get FIVE POINTS!
