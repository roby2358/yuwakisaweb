# Prime Goals

Ideas for the 1000 gold sink — powerful one-time purchases that don't grant an item.

1. **Seal all breaches at once** — pay 1000g to close every open breach on the map
2. **Reveal the full world map** — fog of war cleared permanently
3. **Summon a wildlife army** — spawn 10-15 friendly wildlife that attack chaos creatures
4. **Purify the land** — restore all shattered/distressed hexes in a massive radius
5. **Fortify a village** — upgrade a village to a haven (full rest + shop)
6. **Bribe the Unraveler's minions** — all breach guardians despawn
7. **Golden aura** — permanent +1 to all stats for the rest of the run
8. **Establish a new haven** — place a haven at your current location
9. **Merchant's network** — all shops permanently restock with new items
10. **Bounty board** — gain 2x XP from kills for the rest of the run
11. **Forest communion** — permanent +1 vision radius
12. **Golden harvest** — every village and haven sprouts 5 crops around it
13. **Unlock a 5th skill slot** — equip one more skill
14. **War chest** — permanently gain +2 MP per turn
15. **Hire a scout** — the Maw location is revealed and a safe path highlighted
16. **Mass resurrection** — fully heal and gain +20 max HP permanently
17. **Golden age** — all villages start producing 1 crop per turn passively
18. **Chaos dampener** — breaches stop spawning new enemies for 50 turns
19. **Legendary training** — immediately gain 3 levels worth of stat points
20. **Terraform** — convert a 3-hex radius of water/mountain to plains, opening new paths
