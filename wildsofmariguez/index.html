Wilds of Mariguez

Hecto, a scholar of ancient texts, and Evascor, his stone giant guardian, venture into the Wilds to recover lost artifacts before a rival, Jhirle, claims them first.

Units

Hecto reads scrolls, solves visions, and claims artifacts. Terrain slows him — forests and hills cost 2 MP. He dies if a monster catches him too far from Evascor.

Evascor moves 1 hex per step regardless of terrain and can cross mountains. He automatically fights adjacent monsters each turn, but too many at once will stun him. Three stuns and he falls.

Both share 5 MP per turn. Click a unit to select, click a hex to move.

Artifacts & Scrolls

Scrolls are visible on the map. Walk Hecto over one to reveal a hidden artifact — but Jhirle learns its location one turn later.

The Seer's visions describe terrain near hidden artifacts. Find the matching spot and claim it secretly — Jhirle never learns.

To claim, move Hecto onto the artifact with Evascor within 3 hexes. Costs 2 extra MP. Max 5 in inventory.

Threats

Monsters spawn near Hecto and swarm toward the nearest unit. Hecto hides from them in forest. Evascor fears nothing.

Jhirle appears after your first claim. She races for artifacts you've revealed and must be outrun, blocked, or outsmarted.

Victory

Claim 3 artifacts, then get Hecto to a city on the eastern border with Evascor within 3 hexes.
